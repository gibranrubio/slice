Why this exists

Gifting money is common. Investing that money for the long term is often complicated. Slice is built to make the process clear and straightforward.

One investment option

Slice supports a single S&P 500 tracking fund. There are no other investment choices.

Automatic reinvestment

Dividends paid by the fund are automatically reinvested to purchase additional shares.

Designed for long term holding

Slice is built for holding investments over time, not for active trading.

How it works

The process is designed to be simple and predictable.

Product principles

Slice is built with a small set of fixed rules.

  • One S&P 500 investment option
  • No active trading features
  • Dividend reinvestment enabled by default
  • Clear separation between Slice and the brokerage

For adults and minors

Slice supports individual brokerage accounts for adults and custodial accounts for minors. The appropriate account type is selected during the claim process.
